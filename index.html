Welcome to Campus Bikes

Who we are:

We are Campus Bike & Skate. We are the local resource for cyclists and skaters.

Services that are offered:

Bike Tune Up: $19.99
Have an old bike and been looking to get back out on the trails again? Our experts will tune up your bike so that you are able to ride with ease and be able to focus on the great ride without worrying about your bike's performance.

Custom Repair: $39.99
We understand that accidents will happen. Whether it is something minor like a broken chain or a more serious issue like a bent frame, our experts are able to handle all challenges when it comes to repairing your bike.

Alterations: $29.99
If you want to spice up your bike with a new seat or different pair of rims, our team is able to make a variety of modifications to your bike so that your bike is really your unique bike.
